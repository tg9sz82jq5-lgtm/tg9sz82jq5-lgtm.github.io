E.V.I.

Experimental Video Interface

The E.V.I. consists out of multiple components chained together in a modular setup.
The components are in a transparent shell, to explore the ambiguity of these electrical signals. All the technological components used within are the same as a musical mixing panel, used with dub mixing. All the components use 3.5mm jack inputs. This further solidifies the ambiguity of the sources and allows for video sources to be connected alongside music sources.
The Experimental Video Interface can be made on your own. The documentation below will provide you with the necessary information and files to get started. Happy video editing!
