  • E.V.I. (Experimental Video Interface)
    June 16, 2026

    Tags: Intervention, Experimental, Hardware

    As part of Studio Digital Native, Design Academy Eindhoven

    Featured in the exhibition "Death of the Music Video(?)"

    [...] Experimental Video Interface takes the position of an intervention in the music video industry. The interface consists out of multiple components chained together in a modular setup. These are made to explore the ambiguity of electrical signals used to transfer sound and image. It takes inspiration from dub music, which uses a mixing board like an instrument and revolves around reworking existing music. It is made to bring the personality and emotions of music to a frictionless video editing industry. All components and research are open-sourced through the website found here.
